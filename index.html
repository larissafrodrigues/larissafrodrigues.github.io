About

Larissa Ferreira Rodrigues received the B.Sc. degree in Computer Information Systems from the Universidade Federal de Viçosa, Campus Rio Paranaíba, in 2016 and M.Sc. in Computer Science from the Universidade Federal de Viçosa, Brazil, in 2018. She is a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Universidade Federal de Uberlândia. Her research interests include Image Processing, Computer Vision and Machine Learning.
